Libgdx and Box2DLights

LibGDX is a java-based development library for making your own games on Windows, Mac, Linux, or Android. This particular resource deals with the optional add-on library known as Box2D Lights, based off of the standardized Box2D. This tutorial is great for intermediate programmers wanting to add lighting engines to their games without having to make their own from scratch.
